Some musical training facts

Jansen started studying music at the Dutch Rock Academy in 1999, entering the Conservatorium Tilburg three years later. She studied musical theatre and a year of opera.[19][20] Later, she started teaching performing in her own course. She used to play the guitar, piano and flute and has completed several years of music studies.

Recording facts

Jansen has recorded over 14 albums as the main vocalist with the following bacnds: After forever, Nightwish, Northward, ReVamp and Star One. She has made over 15 guest appearances with: Ayreon, Nightmare, MaYan, Epica, Evergrey and even Nightwish former lead singer - Tarja Turunen.
